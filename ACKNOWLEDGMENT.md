## Customization Guidelines

1. **Choose the style** that matches your project report's tone (formal, detailed, brief, etc.)

2. **Replace all placeholders** with actual names:
   - [Supervisor Name]
   - [Head of Department Name]
   - [Department Name]
   - [University Name]
   - [Your Name]
   - [Your Student ID]
   - [Date]
   - [Friend Names] - if applicable

3. **Religious references**: Keep or remove the references to Allah/God based on your preference

4. **Add specific mentions**: If someone made a particular contribution (provided data, helped with testing, reviewed code), mention them specifically

5. **Length**: Typically 200-400 words is appropriate for an acknowledgment section

6. **Tone**: Should be sincere and appreciative, not overly formal or casual

7. **Order of thanks**: Usually follows this hierarchy:
   - God/Higher power (if applicable)
   - Supervisor/Advisor
   - Department head/Faculty
   - Family (especially parents)
   - Friends/Colleagues
   - Participants/Contributors
   - Others

---

**Note**: The acknowledgment appears after the dedication and before the abstract in your project document. It's a personal section where you express genuine gratitude to those who supported your work.

**British vs American Spelling**: 
- American: Acknowledgment
- British: Acknowledgement

Both are correct - choose based on your institution's preference or your region.
